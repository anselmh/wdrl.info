Hey, I’m Anselm Hannemann and I curated this project for over ten years as an attempt to concatenate all the web development links that pop up every single day so you don’t have to. The project is not active anymore but you can find all links, recommendations still here.

The newsletter was sent to 18.000 subscribers via email, published on the website, feed and on social media to several thousand readers every month, since over ten years and over 300 editions.
The newsletter has featured on SmashingMagazine, CSS-Tricks and other well known industry sites, events and is recommended by many industry-influencers from Google, Adobe, Facebook, …

WDRL was financed entirely by your support. ♥

It’s only possible because I love what I do. I have no advertising in my newsletter and get financed by my readers who love what I do and find it worth a small donation.
